Bean Burrito

Description

This delicous and easy to make bean burrito recipe will make any meal a snap. Starting with an easy bean, rice, cheese , avocado base, you can customize these bad boyz to any pallete. There is nothing tastier or easier than wiping up a classic burrito. So without further to do here is the burrito in its raw form!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry white rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 2 fresh avocados
  • 1 container sour cream
  • 1/2 cup 3 cheese blend/mexican cheese blend
  • 4 white flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Place just under 2 cups of water in a medium pan
  2. Bring water to boil
  3. Pour dry rice into water
  4. Set rice to a simmer
  5. In a second small pan pour out the beans and set burner to low heat
  6. Once water starts to burn off of rice place lid on pan and turn off heat
  7. Cut both Avocados in half
  8. Place tortilla on plate
  9. Cover tortilla in light layer of cheese
  10. Remove rice pot lid and ensure fluffy texture
  11. Put a spoon full of rice and beans on tortilla
  12. Put one half of avocado and drop of sour cream on tortilla
  13. Put another pinch of cheese on top
  14. Tuck in sides of tortilla and roll burrito
  15. Enjoy that awesome burrito!!!
