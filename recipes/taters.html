Air Fried Baked Potatoes

Description

If you have never used an air fryer to make baked potatoes, then you are missing out. These puppies will change your life and they are super easy to whip up. So get ready to take a train to flavor town.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large taters
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Pinhc of garlic powder
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F
  2. Rub taters with olive oil and season with dry ingredients
  3. Cook taters for 40 to 50 minutes or until soft
  4. Slice them suckers lengthwise and drop in a tablespoon of butter in each
  5. Enjoy!
